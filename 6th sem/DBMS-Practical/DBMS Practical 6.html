* Tasks to be performed :

1.Retrieve name in uppercase whose salary is below 15000 and live in Amravati.

 mysql> SELECT UPPER(Ename) FROM employee WHERE (Salary>15000) AND (City="AMARAVTI");

2.Retrieve name in lower case and designation in uppercase whose salary is greater than 10000 and age is 22,25 or 30.

 mysql> SELECT LOWER(EName),upper(Desgination) FROM employee WHERE (Salary>10000) AND age IN(20,25,30);

3.Find out length of designation of employee whose empno 20 and department no. 4

 mysql> SELECT EName,LENGTH(Desgination) AS "Leghth" FROM employee WHERE (EmpNo=20) AND (DeptNo=4);

4.Find total salary of employee who live in Mumbai.

 mysql> SELECT SUM(Salary) FROM employee WHERE city="MUMBAI";

5.Find average salary of employee whose age from 22 to 30.

 mysql> SELECT AVG(Salary) FROM employee WHERE Age BETWEEN 21 AND 30;

6.Count no. of employee whose salary is below 15000 and live in Nagpur.

 mysql> SELECT COUNT(*) AS "Emp Count" FROM employee where (Salary<15000) AND (City="NAGPUR");

7. Find maximum salary that live in Pune and designation as a manager.

 mysql> SELECT MAX(Salary) FROM employee WHERE (City="PUNE") AND (Desgination="Manager");

8.Count no. of employee whose name starts with ‘p’ and having salary is greater than 5000 and less than 20000.

 mysql> SELECT COUNT(*) AS "OUTPUT" FROM employee WHERE (EName like "P%") AND (Salary>5000 AND Salary<20000);

9.Find all data of employee whose salary is less than the average salary.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM employee WHERE Salary < ALL(SELECT AVG(Salary) FROM EMPLOYEE);

10.Find employee id, name, age, city whose age is lowest among all employees.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNo,EName,Age,City FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Age=(SELECT MIN(Age) FROM EMPLOYEE);

11.Find employee id, department no., name, age, city, country and mobile no. whose age is largest among all employees and live in Pune.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNo,DeptNo,EName,Age,City,Country FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Age=(SELECT MAX(Age) FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE City="PUNE");
