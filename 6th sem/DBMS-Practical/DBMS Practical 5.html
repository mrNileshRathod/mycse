* Tasks to be performed :

1.Display employee ID, name, designation, salary and incremented salary which is incremented by 20% to those employee who having salary greater than 10000 and having post as clerk.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNO,EName,Desgination,Salary,(Salary+Salary*0.2) AS 'INC Salary' FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Salary>10000 AND Desgination='Clerk';

2.Calculate double salary of employee from EMPLOYEE who lives in pune.

 mysql> SELECT Salary*2 AS 'Double Salary' FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE City ='Pune';

3.Find name of employee whose salary is not equal to 4000 and equal to 10000.

 mysql> SELECT EName FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Salary!=4000 AND Salary=10000;

4. Retrieve data of employee whose salary is less than 20000 and greater than 5000.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Salary<20000 AND Salary>5000;

5.Retrieve data of employee whose salary is not greater than 7000 (including).

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Salary<=7000;

6.Display employee ID, name, designation, salary whose salary is 4000, 5000, and 10,000.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNO,EName,Desgination,Salary FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Salary IN(4000,5000,10000);

7.Display all data of employee whose salary is not 30000 and 28000.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE (Salary!=30000) AND (Salary!=28000);

8.Retrieve ID, name, age and salary whose age between 18 to 40.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNo,EName,Age,Salary FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Age BETWEEN 18 AND 40;

9.Retrieve ID, name, age and salary whose salary not between 15000 to 30000.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNo,EName,Age,Salary FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Salary NOT BETWEEN 15000 AND 30000;

10.Display data of employee whose name starts with ‘a’ and his city name ends with ‘e’.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE (EName LIKE 'A%') AND (City LIKE '%E');

11.Display all data of employee whose name starts with ‘a’ and his district name not contain character ‘ra’.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE (EName LIKE 'A%') AND (District NOT LIKE '%RA%');

12.Display employee data from employee table whose values are unknown.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Age is null;

13.Retrieve data of employee whose salary is greater than 7000 and designation as a clerk.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE (Salary>7000) AND (Desgination='Clerk');

14.Retrieve employee name, designation and salary of employee, either their salary is greater than 10000 or designation as a clerk.

 mysql> SELECT EName,Desgination,Salary FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE (Salary>10000) OR (Desgination='Clerk');

15.Retrieve data of employee whose salary is not greater than 9000.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE Salary<9000;

16.Write a query to retrieve data as employee no, employee name, salary, city, and country from EMPLOYEE table that lives in Pune and Mumbai and age between 25 to 45.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNo,EName,Salary,City,Country FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE (City='PUNE' OR City='MUMBAI') AND (Age BETWEEN 25 AND 45);
