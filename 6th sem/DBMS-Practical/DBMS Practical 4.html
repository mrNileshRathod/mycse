* Tasks to be performed :

1.Retrieve data from EMPLOYEE table with salary in descending order.

 mysql> SELECT * FROM EMPLOYEE ORDER BY Salary DESC;

2.Display designation, minimum salary, maximum salary, average salary, salary sum of an employee of each designation.

 mysql> SELECT Desgination,MIN(Salary),MAX(Salary),AVG(Salary),SUM(Salary) FROM EMPLOYEE GROUP BY Desgination;

3.Display employee name, dept no, total salary of each department with total salary is greater than 6000.

 mysql> SELECT DeptNo,SUM(Salary) FROM EMPLOYEE GROUP BY DeptNo HAVING SUM(Salary)>60000;

4.Display how many employees are in the table.

 mysql> SELECT COUNT(*) FROM EMPLOYEE;

5.Display average salary in descending order of each department where department salary between 5000 to 10000.

 mysql> SELECT DeptNo,AVG(Salary)  FROM EMPLOYEE GROUP BY DeptNo HAVING AVG(Salary) BETWEEN 5000 and 10000 ORDER BY DeptNo DESC;

6.Find how many employees having same age.

 mysql> SELECT Age,COUNT(*) "Same Age Count" FROM EMPLOYEE GROUP BY Age;

7.Find name, designation, emp no, whose joining before year 2013 and display them in their descending order of their DOJ.

 mysql> SELECT EmpNo,EName,Desgination FROM EMPLOYEE WHERE JDate<'2013-01-01' ORDER BY JDate DESC;

8.Find how many employees from each city.

 mysql> SELECT City,COUNT(*)"City Count" FROM EMPLOYEE GROUP BY City;
